Delicious Salad

Learn how to make the most delicious salad!

Anyone can make it!

Ingredients:

  • iceberg lettuce
  • diced cooked ham
  • onion
  • carrot
  • mushrooms
  • cheese
  • ranch dressing

Steps:

  1. Slice onions. Chop carrots and mushrooms. Chop lettuce.
  2. Mix together in a bowl. Add cheese and ranch dressing.
  3. Enjoy your salad!
