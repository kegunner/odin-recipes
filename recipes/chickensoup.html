Chicken Noodle Soup

Learn how to make the best chicken noodle soup!

Anyone can make it!

Ingredients:

  • cooked chicken
  • pasta noodles
  • carrots
  • celery
  • onion
  • broth
  • water
  • salt
  • pepper
  • garlic salt

Steps:

  1. Chop carrots and celery. Cut onions. Shred chicken.
  2. Put carrots, celery, and onions in a pot over medium-high heat. Cook until soft. Add garlic salt.
  3. Stir in chicken after about a minute. Cook for about two minutes.
  4. Add salt, pepper, water, broth, and basil leaf.
  5. Cook for about 10 min.
  6. Pour in pasta noodles. Cook until soft.
  7. Enjoy!
